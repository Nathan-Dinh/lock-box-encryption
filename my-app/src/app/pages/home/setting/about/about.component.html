About Us

Welcome to Call Lock Box Encryption!


We are thrilled to introduce our innovative web application designed to enhance your privacy and security. Call Lock Box Encryption is a cutting-edge solution that allows you to take pictures and securely store them with robust encryption options. Whether you need to safeguard personal memories, confidential documents, or sensitive information, our app provides a seamless and secure way to protect your data.


Our Story

Call Lock Box Encryption was born out of a passion for privacy and a commitment to providing users with the tools they need to keep their information safe. In a world where data breaches and privacy invasions are all too common, we wanted to create an app that empowers individuals to take control of their personal data.

  • Nathan: With a background in software engineering and a keen interest in cryptography, Nathan leads the development of our encryption algorithms and ensures that our app remains at the forefront of security technology.

Our Technology

We leverage the power of Cordova to port our web application over to Android, providing a consistent and high-quality user experience across devices. Cordova allows us to utilize web technologies such as Angular, while also tapping into native device capabilities, ensuring that our app performs optimally on your Android device. Angular helps us build a dynamic and responsive user interface, enhancing the overall user experience with its powerful framework for building client-side applications.
